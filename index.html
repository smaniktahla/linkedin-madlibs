What is this?

LinkedIn Mad Libs is a satirical fill-in-the-blank generator built around the tired writing patterns that show up on LinkedIn over and over: the humble-brag congratulations, the funding-announcement mission statement, the "I recently had the opportunity to..." reflection, the iceberg breakdown, the federal-contractor buzzword salad. Pick a template below, drop in a few words, and it assembles a post in the exact cadence of the genre it's parodying: a two-minute joke, not a productivity tool. Read more on the About page.
